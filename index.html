Meme-otional

Life is often a blur. It’s difficult to assess the struggles and victories of day to day life. When trying to explain your overall wellness to a medical professional, most people can’t truly express how well they’ve truly been, defaulting to “I’m fine.”

Meme-otional is a phone application designed to solve that problem in a fun and engaging way. Utilizing all components of CRUD functionality, Meme-otional tracks the users mood with gifs of memes. Using react chartsjs 2, all the moods you user feels will be added to a chart that totals the different categories.

Use By

Americans waste food. Wasted food wreaks havoc on the environment and on people's wallets. You're not just adding more methane to the atmosphere from food going into landfills. You're also losing out on that hiking trip to Iceland you've been planning for years but never quite had enough money for.

Use By is a phone application designed to elimate food waste and save the user money. It was created during the Nash Hack Weekathon held in October 2018.

Though I was only able to pair program because of computer issues, I personally assisted in the creation of the navbar component. I also participated in the early drafting process of the app, attending the consulting sessions prior to the 48 hour hackathon held over the weekend after the consulting sessions.
